VNN Live Podcast

Monds November 21, 2016 Community

As most of you know, we have been doing news reviews on our channel for some time now. However, a news review is meant to be short and to the point. VNN would like to add some spice to this by going a step further and making a podcast. Don’t worry though we will still do our news reviews as well!

How will we proceed?

Every Saturday evening (EST) we will hold a podcast. (Time and day may change as time goes). Here, we will discuss and give our thoughts on many things happening in the voice synth world such as the following:

  • Events
  • News
  • Highlights
  • Answer questions that the fans may have. (We may not be able to answer all of your questions for a variety of reasons but we will try to answer as much as we can).
  • And much more!

With that being said, if you have any questions you would like to ask, you can send us a message on any of our media. We will prepare these questions ahead of time. This podcast will also be live for those of you who want to ask questions during the podcast. The exact time will be announced a few hours before it begins so people can get ready. Keep in mind that this is the basics and things may change as time goes. We hope you enjoy!
