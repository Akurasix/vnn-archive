The Hatsune Miku x Ange Vierge Collab Event Commences!

marumain September 14, 2017 Events, News Leave a comment 3,009 Views

Just as we previously covered, the Hatsune Miku collab event with Ange Vierge ~Girls Battle~ is now live in the popular mobile RPG from SEGA! Already making airwaves on Japanese television are four commercials commemorating the start of the collab, which can also be watched on SEGA Games Official Mobile App channel on YouTube!

Collab Details

Prologue

One day, Miku is awakened by a voice saying “You must heal her…” From there, she encounters two other girls named Kagamine Rin and Megurine Luka, both of whom, like Miku, have lost their memories and can recall nothing but their own names. Not knowing who they are or why they are here, Rin and Luka merely explain “We’ve come to this world in order to heal a certain ‘her’ with our song…”

Miku and her new friends then find themselves on the mysterious Seiran Island. Guided by that voice saying “You must heal her…,” they encounter the school’s Public Morals Committee, determined to find out what the mysterious voice is calling them to do…

(From Famitsu and the official site)

Game Details

From September 15th to the 22nd, there will be a special level in the game featuring Miku, Rin, and Luka, complete with original character panel illustrations and special gacha to unlock. Each character will even be fully voiced for their dialog portions.

Just by logging into the game during the collab period, players can unlock the special gacha featuring Miku in a Seiran Academy school uniform for free. Also revealed are the gacha illustrations for Rin, Luka, and even Miku in her Code Ω 00 Euphilia outfit!
