Mitchie M “Virtual Popstar” Album Released

Monds November 5, 2019 Merchandise, News Leave a comment 3,482 Views

Mitchie M’s newest album feat. Hatsune Miku has now been released and is titled “Virtual Popstar“. The artwork on the album was illustrated by Haruhiko Mikimoto who is the designer for the famous Macross series!

 

Album

There are several options for purchasing the album.

Cd Japan

Regular Edition: Includes the album only, for 2,700 JPY and can be purchased here.

Limited Edition: Includes the album and a DVD for 3,939 JPY and can be purchased here.

Both the above include a tin badge that is randomly selected among a set of 6.

 

Limited Edition with Vinyl: Includes the album and a vinyl for 4,000 JPY. You purchase the vinyl version here. It also includes a bonus A4-sized clear folder.

You can also purchase the album on other platforms as well, although you will need a forwarding service for these as they are Japanese websites.

Amazon JP

You can purchase the regular edition here for 2,970 JPY and the limited edition here for 4,400 JPY. This version includes a mouse pad as a bonus.

Tower Records

You can purchase the regular edition here for 2,970 JPY and the limited edition here for 4,333 JPY. This version includes a B2 calendar poster as a bonus.

Animate

You can purchase the regular edition here for 2,970 JPY and the limited edition here for 4,332 JPY. This version includes an A3 clear poster as a bonus.

Tsutaya

You purchase the regular edition here for 2,970 JPY and the limited edition here for 4,332 JPY. This version includes a mini colored paper as a bonus.
