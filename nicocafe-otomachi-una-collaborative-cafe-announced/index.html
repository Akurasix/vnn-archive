Nicocafe x Otomachi Una Collaborative Cafe Announced!

Spookie May 15, 2019 Events, News

Food and music are probably the best combination of them all, and why don’t mix things together with a collaborative Nicocafe with Otomachi Una? Her music will make an awesome mix with such delicious plates and drinks!

Our beloved eel-themed virtual singer will join the party with her special Nicocafe starting from May 21st to June 3rd. She will not not only bring her joyful energy to the cafe with some of her song turned into plates and drinks, but also some adorable merchandise for all of her fans’ enjoyment! Let’s give them a look together!

Drink Menu

We can’t call a cafe a real one until he offer us some nice drinks, don’t you think? Una of course wouldn’t want her guests to feel thirsty, so she gave us an awesome menù!

Each drink costs 700 JPY and, at exception of the first one, is actually based and titled off of some of Una’s most popular songs! Here is the full list of them all!

  • Otomachi Una’s Spicy Drink – (Whip Cream / Butterfly Pea Tea / Pie / Lemon / Jelly)
  • “I’ll Make Kanden” by Carlos Hakamada – (Blue Curacao Syrup / Tapioca / Chocolate / Soda / Whip Cream / Popping Candy)
  • “Like A Love Letter of 365 Days” by mint* – (Soda / Lavender Syrup / Blue Curacao Syrup / Lemon Syrup / Sprinkles / Argent)
  • “Dancing Mr. Dinosaur” by HihumiP – (Milk / Matcha Tea / Pie Whip Cream / Edible Flower)
  • “Prettinkle Sister” by LamazeP – (Strawberry Syrup / Cow Milk / Whip Cream / Wafer)
  • “Hate it! Hate it! Huge Ego!” by KurageP – (Violet Syrup / Soda / Coconut Gel / Whip Cream)

Awesome menu isn’t it? You can also order a drink set, priced 1,200 JPY, that will get you a special A4 clear file folder! (It gets chosen randomly)

Plates

Food is of course there as well, featuring lots of creativity thanks to their reference to some of Una’s popular songs!

  • Sweetly Delicious! Una Parfait – 950 JPY
  • Let’s freely draw! “I wanna be-!” Pancake (Based off of “I wanna be it!” by kinoshita) – 1,200 JPY
  • “ever” plate (Based off of “ever” by Cat Allergy)- 1,100 JPY
  • “I can’t be satisfied with one bite!” Dessert Plate (Based off of “Booo!” by TOKOTOKO) – 950 JPY
  • “Let’s eat a cow (or beef)!” Stamina beef bowl (Based off of “Ushinohi☆Abduction” by cosMo – 1,200 JPY
  • “Grrr!” Beast Pasta (Based off of “Beast Dance” by KurageP) – 1,200 JPY

All of those delicious plates look so much tasty! I would love to try them out myself as well!

Goods

We have a lot of merchandise too of course! All of those goods will be available at nicocafe’s location during the collaboration with Una!

There is something for anyone’s taste, from buttons to some adorable acrylic stands. Let’s have a look!

  • Big Acrylic Stand (Height: 273mm, width: 231mm) – 4,000 JPY
  • Acrylic Stands (6 Variant) – 800 JPY Each
  • Acrylic Keychains with Stands (3 Variants) – 700 JPY Each
  • Buttons (3 Variants) – 400 JPY Each
  • Tote-bag – 1,500 JPY

All prices don’t include VAT fees!

Our little virtual idol gave her best for those goods and it shows. If you manage to attend you should really grab something to bring home!

Special Paper Coasters

Last but not least, there are extra gifts to everyone that orders either a plate or a drink. That’s right! You will get a special paper coaster for each order you make!

There are 13 different designs in total and they are giving them out randomly for each drink and plate you order there.

Will you able to get your favorite ones? Who knows!
