Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collection Launches on WeLoveFine!

Hoshi August 31, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News Leave a comment 2,430 Views

WeLoveFine​​ ​has​ ​partnered​ ​with​ ​Crypton​ ​Future​ ​Media​ ​to launch​ ​the​ ​official​ ​Hatsune​ ​Miku​ ​10th​ ​Anniversary Fan-Created​ ​Collection!​ ​​ ​This​ ​is​ ​a​ ​unique​ ​opportunity​ ​for fans​ ​of​ ​Hatsune​ ​Miku​ ​to​ ​get​ ​directly​ ​involved​ ​in​ ​the celebration​ ​by​ ​creating​ ​officially-licensed​ ​merchandise. This​ ​new​ ​line​ ​of​ ​products,​ ​features​ ​designs​ ​from independent​ ​artists,​ ​voted​ ​on​ ​and​ ​curated​ ​through​ ​the Hatsune​ ​Miku​ ​Fan​ ​Forge​.

About Fan Forge

The Hatsune Miku Fan forge, which we previously reported on, was created in celebration of Miku’s 10th Anniversary. While designs featuring all Crypton characters were welcome, a special emphasis was put on Miku’s big 10th. Submissions closed quite some time ago and some designs were turned into T-shirts. And, now, we have even more designs for shirts, sweaters, and even bags!

There are tons of Miku 10th designs as well as regular Fan Forge designs. Be sure to check out We Love Fine to get your Miku merch fix!

The Designs

Listed below are the new designs featured on the special 10th Anniversary Celebration page. You can click any image that you like, to head to We Love Fine and see all the products featuring it!

In addition, the end of each section will have a list of what special items each design is available on. Unless otherwise stated, every design is available as a t-shirt. Shirts are available in both men and women’s sizes, so, you’ll be sure to be able to grab the perfect fit.

Please note that these products are made to order, which means they won’t ship immediately after purchase. However, they’re all so amazing that they’re definitely worth waiting for!

Special Features

These designs are shown at the very top of the 10th Anniversary page and are beautifully featured!

The “Love Miku” design is available as a bag and a sweater as well as shirts.

Special Anniversary Collection Designs

Available as a Bag: “Neon Miku“, “Thank U For 10 Years“, “Loading Hatsune Miku“, and “Sing a Song”

Fan Forge Designs

Available as a Bag: “Miku Links the World“, “Blooming”
Available as a Sweater: “Blooming”
Available as an Art Print: “Blooming”

About We Love Fine

WeLoveFine​​ ​​is​ ​a​ ​merchandise​ ​distributor​ ​created​ ​for​ ​and powered​ ​by​ ​fans.​ ​They​ ​give​ ​independent​ ​artists​ ​the​ ​unique opportunity​ ​to​ ​gather​ ​and​ ​submit​ ​their​ ​art​ ​as​ ​merchandise ideas.​ ​Top​ ​designs​ ​are​ ​selected​ ​and​ ​released​ ​as officially-licensed​ ​products,​ ​and​ ​every​ ​sale​ ​generates revenue​ ​for​ ​the​ ​designer​ ​and​ ​promotion​ ​for​ ​their​ ​brand. They ​are​ ​home​ ​to​ ​the​ ​most​ ​creative​ ​licensed​ ​designs​ ​on​ ​the web.​ Their ​site​ ​includes​ ​clothing​ ​and​ ​accessories​ ​featuring fan-favorite​ ​and​ ​even​ ​fan-created​ ​designs​ ​from​ ​My​ ​Little Pony,​ ​Hatsune​ ​Miku,​ ​Steven​ ​Universe,​ ​Over​ ​The​ ​Garden Wall,​ ​Homestuck​ ​and​ ​more!
