“VOCALOID Yumemi Nemu” Bonus Store Items Revealed!

marumain June 29, 2017 Merchandise, News

The release day for Yumemi Nemu‘s upcoming compilation album, VOCALOID Yumemi Nemu, is almost here! Check out its latest trailer!

To further whet your appetite for its impending release, a number of stores have announced bonus items which customers who purchase the album at their location can receive!

Review Letter from Nemu Yumemi

This letter from Nemu Yumemi (the person) to Yumemi Nemu (the VOCALOID) gives her review on all of the songs included in the album. It also features a drawing of Yumemi Nemu (the VOCALOID) by Nemu (the person) and her own personal signature!
