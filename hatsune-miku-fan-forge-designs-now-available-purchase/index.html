Hatsune Miku Fan Forge Designs Now Available for Purchase!

Hoshi August 1, 2017 Merchandise, News Leave a comment 2,713 Views

Fans who have been following We Love Fine and VNN may have seen the news about the massive Hatsune Miku Fan Forge on We Love Fine. While the website has done Miku contests in the past, this is by far the largest one yet. And, despite the name saying “Miku,” all Crypton VOCALOIDs are welcome! So far, there are 7 designs available on We Love Fine, and a special 8th one on Tokyo Otaku Mode!

The Designs

While most designs feature Miku, there’s also a Rin T-shirt available, as well as one featuring the whole Crypton crew. All the shirts are incredible and definitely worth buying! Each one is available in Men’s and Women’s sizes and priced at $25 USD, except for the Vox Populi shirt which is on sale for $23.74 at Tokyo Otaku Mode. All shirts except for the Vox Populi one, which is set to release in September, are Made-To-Order.
