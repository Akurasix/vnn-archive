VOLKS USA Hosting Limited Web Event for VOCALOID Dollfie Dream Dolls!

Hoshi April 5, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News Leave a comment 4,701 Views

In celebration of ANIME MATSURI 2017, VOLKS USA is hosting a limited web event. Fans who missed out on the initial orders for Dollfie Dream Hatsune Miku, Dollfie Dream Sister Kagamine Rin, and Dollfie Dream Sister Kagamine Len, now have a chance to grab them! In addition to the dolls being for sale, there are quite a few costumes as well! All sales will begin on April 7th, 2017, and last until April 17th, 2017. However, these VOCALOID Dollfie Dream Dolls are limited. Grab them before they’re gone!

In addition, the dolls will be available at ANIME MATSURI 2017 in Houston, Texas. So if you’re at the convention, you can grab them there as well!

For more information, be sure to follow the VOLKS USA Facebook page as well as the ANIME MATSURI 2017 celebration page.
