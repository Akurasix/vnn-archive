Hatsune Miku x StraStella Collaboration Now Live!

marumain November 4, 2017 Events, News

For those who can’t get enough of Hatsune Miku‘s appearances in mobile RPGs, the previously reported collaboration with NEXON Korea Corporation‘s sci-fi strategy RPG, StraStella, is now live!

Those who are learning about the game for the first time can check out the promotional video below.

Collaboration Details

Story

At an ancient archeological site, excavators have discovered a mysterious piece of software.

After much hardship, they were able to reactivate this ancient software, a digital life form known as “Hatsune Miku.”

For her own reasons, Miku has decided that she will be staying aboard the Asgard Warship. However…

In-game Appearance

In StraStella, Miku will be appearing as a new “Pixie,” an artificial life form whom players can interact and foster a relationship with. Players can acquire Miku by completing the limited-time quest called “Hearts Connected Through Song.” By communicating with Miku and giving her presents, players can deepen their bond with her, and eventually cause her to “awaken.” Doing so will also unlock a special live performance with Miku singing and dancing to the StraStella theme song, “Shooting Star!” Here are all of the songs that she can sing in the game, and how to earn them:

  • “Shooting Star”: Get Miku to “awaken”
  • “My Universe“: Grant Miku’s request for a present (fulfilled at random)
  • “Smile”: Purchase with 300 gems
  • “Jumon wo Tonaeru yo (呪文を唱えるよ)”: Purchase with 300 gems

In battle, her special awakening skill is called Pure Voice, which restores the HP for all allies around her, and raises the critical hit rate for a target ally.

Special Miku-Themed Suits

Speaking of battle, Miku will even be able to pilot her own special armored suit called the HARMONIA! It, and the other five special limited-time suit units – the CRESCENT, the ARPEGGIO, the FORTISSIMO, the PIANISSIMO, and FANTASIA – are all unsurprisingly modeled after Miku herself! They can each be unlocked as ultra-rare gacha.

HARMONIA (Support Type)

Skills:

  • Double Attack 1: Repeatedly deals damage equal to 30% of your attack power to an enemy
  • Defensive Heal 5: Restores an ally’s HP by 400% of your attack power and raises their defense by 35% for 2 turns
  • Restarting 5: Grants another turn to units who have already taken an action and increases their movement range by 2 spaces for one turn

 

CRESCENT (Assault Type)

Skills:

  • Burning Attack 2: Deals damage equal to 110% of your attack power to an enemy. Reduces the target’s MP by 30%, and deals additional damage equal to the amount reduced
  • Offensive Dash 6: Leaps towards an enemy in a straight line and deals damage equal to 240% of your attack power. Raises your attack power by 25% for 2 turns
  • Acid Burst 6: Deals damage equal to 150% of your attack power to all enemies surrounding singing units. Inflicts corrosion status that causes continuous damage and reduces the target’s evasion rate

ARPEGGIO (Bombardment Type)

Skills:

  • Fire Attack 2: Deals damage equal to 110% of your attack power. If your target is stunned, you deal additional damage equal to 55% of your attack power
  • Stun Missile 6: Deals damage equal to 160% of your attack power to all enemies surrounding your target and stuns them for 1 turn
  • Cripple Resist 6: Deals damage equal to all enemies surrounding your target equal to 185% of your attack power and reduces their resistance by 60%

FORTISSIMO (Sniper Type)

Skills:

  • Normal Attack 2: Deals damage equal to 130% of your attack power to all enemies in front of you
  • Poison Check 6: Deals damage equal to 180% of your attack power to all enemies diagonal to your target. If your target has the corrosion status, you deal additional damage equal to 55% of your attack power
  • Cripple Resist 6: Deals damage equal to all enemies surrounding your target equal to 185% of your attack power and reduces their resistance by 60%

 

PIANISSIMO (Support Type)

Skills:

  • Fire Attack 2: Deals damage equal to 110% of your attack power. If your target is stunned, you deal additional damage equal to 55% of your attack power
  • Healing 6: Restores an ally’s HP by 440% of your attack power
  • Increase Power 6: Raises the damage that all allies deal with critical strikes by 50% for 2 turns

FANTASIA

Skills:

  • Double Attack 2: Repeatedly deals damage equal to 40% of your attack power to an enemy
  • Defensive Heal 6: Restores the HP of all allies within a specified range by 200% of your attack power and raises their defense by 50% for 2 turns
  • Restarting Project 6: Grants another turn to units who have already taken an action, and protects them from taking damage up to 3 times for 2 turns

 

Hatsune Miku Outfits

Finally, just by logging into the game during the collaboration period (October 31st ~ November 28th), players will instantly unlock Miku’s outfits for four of the game’s characters. You can unlock the Miku outfit for Reas during the first week of the collab (started 10/31), for Puris during the second week (11/7), for Jini during the third week (11/14), and for Rabi during the fourth and final week (11/21). (Note: these Romanized names are not official)

Furthermore, a special Miku Outfit Package is available for purchase for ￥2,000 JPY (about $17.50 USD) for the rest of the game’s characters, which also includes special SSS-Grade Mini-Mini units.
