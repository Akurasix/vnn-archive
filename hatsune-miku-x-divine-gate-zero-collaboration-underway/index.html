Hatsune Miku x Divine Gate ZERO Collaboration Underway!

marumain October 17, 2017 Events, News

A brand new collaboration between Hatsune Miku and Divine Gate ZERO (ディバインゲート零), the mobile panel battle RPG from GungHo Online Entertainment, is now live!

The new collab was first announced back on September 23rd through a special livestream announcement on Dengeki Online’s official YouTube channel, which can be watched below!

Story

About one year has passed since Shibuya City by rocked by the Demonic Shadow Eclipse, which swallowed up everything in its path, including 30% of the population on Earth.

At what was the epicenter of the chaos, the site of the Shibuya Rock Festival, volunteers are now rebuilding and reconstructing. They are preparing to hold another festival with the theme “Breathing Life Back Into The World Through The Power of Music.”

Among the multitude of artists who will be attending the festival, there is one certain diva who has confirmed her participation.

“May my song reach everyone in the world, and may it serve as a symbol of courage and hope.”

The name of the participating diva who read these words is none other than Hatsune Miku.

The festival organization committee is incredibly enthusiastic about holding this festival around Miku’s appearance, intent on turning it into that “symbol of courage and hope.”

However…

Collaboration Units

In the initial livestream announcement, illustrations for the new evolved forms of existing units, brand new units, a new quest boss, and mission reward that will all be part of the collab were all revealed (please note that the following Romanized or translated names, titles, profiles, and skills are not official).

The Lad Who Sings With Spirit: Kagamine Len
Evolved Unit – Cheerful Singer: Kagamine Len

Profile: This blond lad understands full well that there is much unhappiness and absurdity in this world. But despite that, he always puts on a strong and forward-looking face, and sings with a smile so wide that his pearly whites are always visible, knowing that it helps brighten up the day for those around him.

“Whenever life gets you down, just listen to me sing! You’ll feel better right away and find yourself singing along!”

  • Leader Skill: Cheerful Singer
    Increases the attack power of an ally Light-attribute unit by 3x
  • Active Skill: Cheerful Voice
    All panels in your hand become Light-attribute panels, and your hand management time is extended by 1 second for one turn, up to a maximum of 9 seconds (Costs 1 SP)
  • Normal Skill: Fresh Singing
    Charms an enemy unit with a Light-attritube song
  • Passive Skill: Happiness Song
    Increases the chances for a Boost to occur in a slot, affecting up to 2 slots

The Maiden Who Sings With Love: Megurine Luka
Evolved Unit – Compassionate Singer: Megurine Luka

Profile: With a pained expression on her face, she sings her song up towards the sky, all in hopes that her voice will reach someone, even someone whose name she doesn’t know. To this day, carrying all the sentiments of her heart, her voice continues to bring love to somebody’s life.

“If it’s okay, would you like to listen to me sing? If my song can leave a lasting impression in your heart, then that would make me very happy.”

  • Leader Skill: Compassionate Singer
    Increases the HP of an ally Machine-type unit by 2x, and restores HP for all ally units every turn
  • Active Skill: Compassionate Voice
    Reduces the damage taken from enemy units by 80% for 3 turns (Costs 1 SP)
  • Normal Skill: Misty Singing
    Restores 15% HP to all ally units
    Boost: Restores an additional 5% HP to all ally units
  • Passive Skill: Wonder Song
    Slightly raises the chance for a Restorative panel to appear

The Beauty Who Sings With Passion: MEIKO
Evolved Unit – Passionate Singer: MEIKO

Profile: No matter what age we live in, those who forget their passion can never live full lives, and her voice has the power to pick up the fragments of a broken passion. Old or young, male or female, when you’re standing before her, she’ll make you sweat hard and pump your fist in the air.

“It’s no fun wearing a solemn face all the time, right? Then today’s the day to listen to me sing and forget all your troubles! Alright, let’s do this thing!”

  • Leader Skill: Passionate Singer
    Increases the HP of an ally Machine-type unit by 3x
  • Active Skill: Passionate Voice
    Restores 8 SP
  • Normal Skill: Sweet Singing
    Restores 15% HP to all ally units
    Boost: Restores an additional 5% HP to all ally units
  • Passive Skill: Beauty Song
    Slightly raises the chance for a Fire panel to appear

The Man Who Sings of Dreams: KAITO
Evolved Unit – Ambitious Singer: KAITO

Profile: When this young man starts to sing softly, just close your eyes and  let your thoughts take form one by one, taking in each melody one note at a time. Whenever he finishes singing, he’s always met with a maelstrom of applause, to which he responds with a modest bow.

“Although I’m singing for all of you, I’m also singing for each and every one of you individually. It would bring me joy to leave a lasting impression in each of your hearts.”

  • Leader Skill: Ambitious Singer
    Increases the HP of an ally Machine-type unit by 2x, and increases their attack power by 2.5x
  • Active Skill: Ambitious Voice
    Increases Water-attribute damage dealt to all enemy units by 1.5x for 5 turns, and reduces the skills for an ally unit for 5 turns
  • Normal Skill 1: Powerful Singing
    Charms an enemy unit with a Water-attribute song
  • Normal Skill 2: Wild Singing
    Severely charms all enemy units with a Water-attribute song (CRT+13%)

Girl Who Sings With Affection: Kagamine Rin
Evolved Unit – Sweet Singer: Kagamine Rin

Profile: This blond lass always wears a cute smile upon her face. She sings while hopping up and down with her tiny frame, getting others to smile and cheer along, believing that you can make someone happy by cheering for them.

“Let’s all sing together in one big voice! I’m gonna sing my loudest so that I won’t to lose to any one of you! Alright, here we go!”

  • Leader Skill: Sweet Singer
    Increases the HP and attack power of an ally Light-attribute unit by 2.5x
  • Active Skill: Sweet Voice
    Clears all slots, and increases the attack power of a Machine-type unit by 2x (Costs 1 SP)
  • Normal Skill 1: Pretty Singing
    Charms an enemy unit with a Light-attribute song
  • Normal Skill 2: Cute Singing
    Restores 20% HP to all ally units

Digital Diva: Hatsune Miku
Evolved Unit – Diva of Hope: Hatsune Miku

Profile: She’s able to sing skillfully in any genre, and her singing has the power to awaken the innate courage and hope within each person’s heart. Before anyone knew it, she was christened with the title of the legendary diva.

“As long as my singing can help someone, I will continue to sing. To me, there is no greater joy to be had than that…”

  • Leader Skill: Diva of Hope
    Increases the HP of ally Human-type and Machine-type units by 3x, and their attack power by 2x
    When a Wind-attribute Normal Skills takes affect, attack power is increased by 2x
  • Active Skill: Voice of Hope
    When all slots get a Boost, your hand management time is extended by 3 seconds (up to a maximum of 9 seconds) for 99 turns (Costs 4 SP)
  • Normal Skill 1: Lovely Singing
    Slightly confuses an enemy unit with a Wind-attribute song
  • Normal Skill 2: Miracle Singing
    Makes an enemy unit do the Miku Miku with a Wind-attribute song
    Boost: Set a Wind-attribute panel in any two slots

Diva: Hatsune Miku Append
Evolved Unit – Rainbow-Colored Voice: Hatsune Miku Append

Profile: The diva has evolved even further, gaining a new voice, and a new identity, all to sing beautiful songs for us, believing that they can serve as a source of strength.

“No matter how much technology advances, no matter how much we grow, there are still things that we must never forget. To me, that which I must never forget is the will to sing.”

  • Leader Skill: Rainbow-Colored Singer
    Increases the HP of an ally Neutral-attribute or Machine-type unit by 3x, and its attack power by 2.5x
    When Wind-attribute Normal Skills takes affect, attack power is increased by 2x
  • Active Skill: Rainbow-Colored Voice
    Restores 100% HP for all ally units, and removes all status effects and ailments
    Reduces the damage taken by enemy units by 100% for 1 turn (Costs 1 SP)
  • Normal Skill 1: Solid Singing
    Restores 8% HP for all ally units
  • Normal Skill 2: Vivid Singing
    Severely charms an enemy unit with a Neutral-attribute song
    Boost: Set a Neutral-attribute panel in any slot

New Unit – Eternal Diva: Hatsune Miku V3

Profile: The diva continues to evolve, and her voice colors the world in fun colors, sad colors, colors of passion, colors of joy, and more. She beautifies the world just by being in it and singing.

“I will always sing for you, for all of you who are waiting eagerly to hear my song. So now, let’s sing together.”

  • Leader Skill: Eternal Diva
    Increases the HP of ally Light-attribute, Machine-type, and Fairy-type units by 3x, and their attack power by 2.5x
    When units of at least 3 different attributes attack together, their attack power is increased by 2x
  • Active Skill: Eternal Voice
    Reduces the HP of all enemy units by 25%, and increases all damage dealt to enemy units by 2x for 1 turn
  • Normal Skill 1: Lasting Song
    Confuses all enemy units with a Light-attribute song
    Special: All ally units with their defense increased also gain CRT+50%
  • Normal Skill 2: Eternal Song
    Severely charms an enemy unit with a Light-attribute song (CRT+3%)
    Boost: Changes the three leftmost panels in your hand into into Light-, Wind-, and Neutral-attribute panels

Quest Boss – Holun: TypeM

Mission Reward Unit – Symbol of Courage: Miku and Runi

Proile: Runi was pleasantly surprised when she got an offer from the diva herself. Wearing her own handmade outfit, this little diva found herself standing next to and singing alongside the real deal. While doing so, she no longer looked like the brainiac that we all know, but instead just looked like a normal girl having a good time.

“Hehe, it feels a little weird to be wearing the same outfit and singing with her, but I guess two heads are more fun than one, even when it comes to music.”

  • Leader Skill: Symbol of Courage
    Increases the HP and attack power of Human-type and Machine-type ally units by 3x
  • Active Skill: Voice of Courage
    Increases the attack power of an ally Light-attribute unit by 2x for 1 turn
  • Normal Skill 1: Twin Vocal: Ver2
    Charms an enemy unit with a Light-attribute song
  • Normal Skill 2: Let’s All Sing Together
    Severely charms all enemy units with a Light-attribute song
    Boost: Changes the two rightmost panels in your hand into into Light-attribute panels

Gameplay Videos

Later on, 宮坊 (Miyabo) and さあや (Saaya) of Famitsu App sat down with Mister☆Divine himself to try out the new collaboration units in Super-Level (with all units at Level 1) and God-Level (all units at Level 20) challenges. You can see them in action in the below video on Famitsu App’s official YouTube Channel!

The video also reveals several Miku songs that will be used as background music during the collaboration missions:

  • 共鳴エレクトロニカ (Kyoumei Electronica) by しのＰ (ShinoP)
  • 始まりの五線譜 (Hajimari no Gosenfu) by tatsuo and キョータ (Kyota)
  • HATSUSHI by Inagi

And Famitsu App wasn’t alone. Several other video game channels also got the chance to meet with Mister☆Divine to demonstrate and explain the skill sets for the new collaboration units!

For even more gameplay and introduction videos, check out Divine Gate ZERO‘s official YouTube channel!

Missions

During the collaboration period, a series of missions will be made available for players to clear and earn special rewards, including “Hatsune Miku Points.” These special points can be used to purchase the Eternal Diva: Hatsune Miku V3 unit and more!

The first mission is entitled “Hatsune Miku: Episode 1,” and is available from October 13th through November 6th.
The second mission is entitled “Hatsune Miku: Episode 2,” and will be available from October 23rd through November 6th

Each mission will also have an “EX” version which players can complete on Super-Level or God-Level difficulty levels for the chance to earn some Runi: Type M units!

Other Information

Divine Gate ZERO is available for download on the App Store and Google Play, with the collab with Hatsune Miku running from now until November 6th. You can follow Mister☆Divine on Twitter to keep track of all the updates throughout the whole collboration period!
