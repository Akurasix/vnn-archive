Unison League X Hatsune Miku Magical Mirai 2017 Collab And Sweepstakes Begins!

Hoshi October 5, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News

Fans following Unison League will likely remember the Magical Mirai 2016 collaboration last year as well as the Sakura Miku collab that happened just this April. Well, Ateam has no plans of stopping now! Miku has returned to Unison League’s world to celebrate Magical Mirai 2017 alongside her friends. The Unison League x Hatsune Miku Magical Mirai 2017 Collaboration has begun and will last until October 19th at 1:59 Unison Time (1:59 a.m. Eastern Time).

*All links can be found at the end of the article*

In addition to the in-game events that comes with all collabs, this one is stepping things up a notch. You can obtain real Miku merchandise that can’t be obtained outside of Japan! This sweepstakes gives fans a chance to obtain some hard to get Miku merch as well as in-game gems!

The Sweepstakes

Ateam is holding a special sweepstakes to celebrate the Hatsune Miku Magical Mirai 2017 collab!

 

The lucky winners will receive Hatsune Miku merchandise that can’t be obtained outside of Japan or Gems!

 

Merchandise Prizes

  • Magical Mirai 2017 Happi (1 winner)
  • Hatsune Miku Stamp Set (2 winners)
  • Hatsune Miku Pouch (2 winners)

Gem Prizes

  • 39 Gems (10 winners)
  • 25 Gems (15 winners)
  • 10 Gems (30 winners)

Gem winners will be drawn from those who did not win merchandise.

Eligible Players

Unfortunately, the merchandise will only be available to players that reside in the United States of America, Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau. However, Ateam will do their best to ensure that players from more countries can enter in future sweepstakes.

In addition, ALL players can enter. Those who don’t win merchandise can win Gems which are available to anyone who plays Unison League!

Entry Period

Entries are open until Tuesday, October 12th at 1:59 a.m. Unison Time (10/12 at 1:59 Eastern Time)

Winners will be drawn on Tuesday, October 17th.

How to Enter

For the full terms and conditions can be found in game. In addition, the entry form will also be available in-game via a link. So be sure to enter!

In-Game Events

Of course, there’s a lot to do in Unison League as well. From a free gift to quests and even special Spawns, you’re sure to be busy during this collab!

Free Gift

All players who log in between now and the collaboration end time will receive special Magical Mirai 2017 Hatsune Miku hair completely free! This item is limited to one per player and the item is a cosmetic item, meaning it won’t provide any in-game bonuses. But you can expect a lot of players to be sporting their Miku hair during and after the collab!

Exclusive Quest: “Magical Mirai 2017“!

An exclusive Quest based on the Magical Mirai 2017 stage is available. Similar to other Miku collab quests, Hatsune Miku will accompany you, and perform as a special unit. When you perform a Unison Attack, she will cheer you on by singing the song “39” by sasakure.UK and DECO*27.

By using Collaboration Spawn Tickets, which you get from clearing

Quests, you can get your hands on exclusive items.

In addition, you can obtain the Event Reward “Magical Mirai 2017 Hatsune Miku” unit via completing the quest.

Character Name: [Songstress] MM2017 Hatsune Miku

Cost: 32

Element: Water

Unison Skill: Song for the Future

Unison Skill Effects:

  • Ability Power 180.
  • Damages all enemies 4 times with magic water damage at a success of 100 points.
  • Ability Power boosted by 5 for each increase in Skill level.
  • Reduces physical and magic damage taken by all allies by 40% for 40 seconds.
  • Removes status ailments from all allies.
  • Ability Power 70. Heals the HP of all allies.
  • Increases the Cost recovery speed of all allies for 60 seconds.

 

Original Yukata Hatsune Miku Steps on Stage + Unit Spawns

A new collaboration unit is taking center stage in special Spawns. Yukata Miku, clothed in a beautiful Yukata alongside a new Rin and Luka unit, are appearing in special Spawns. Additionally, previous Hatsune Miku collaboration event characters Hatsune Miku, Megurine Luka, Kagamine Rin, and Kagamine Len are making a comeback. For those who missed out, now is your chance to get these exclusive characters.

In addition, there is a one-time exclusive Spawn that guarantees a collaboration UR item. You’ll also have a chance to roll one time on the collaboration Spawn every day for free. Be sure to log in and roll daily!
