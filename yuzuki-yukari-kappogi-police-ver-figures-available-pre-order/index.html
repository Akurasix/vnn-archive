Yuzuki Yukari Kappōgi & Police Ver. Figures Available for Pre-Order Again!

marumain July 22, 2017 Merchandise, News Leave a comment 2,076 Views

Figure company Pulchra announced on their official blog that they are now taking pre-orders for the soon to be re-released Yuzuki Yukari Kappōgi Ver. and Yuzuki Yukari Police Ver. 1/8 scale figures!

Kappōgi Ver. Details

The figure is based on Yukari’s Kappōgi (割烹着) or “cooking apron” design. This design is from a project called “Bell Focus” where Yukari waits on customers instead of a human. For more information on Bell Focus and this design, check out our article on the life-sized version of this figure. This is a resin cast figure and not the usual mass produced kind.

Prototype Makers: Moineau

Price: ￥13,800 JPY (about $138.00 USD) before tax, ￥14,904 (about $149.04 USD) after tax

Pre-order Period: July 19th, 2017 12:00 PM (JST) ~ August 7th, 2017 11:59 PM (JST)

Release date: Sometime in mid-August

Pre-order Links:

Pulchra Online Shop (Please note we’re linking to the Japanese pages for technical reasons. An English version of the website is available but it’s incomplete.)

AHS Online Shop

Police Ver. Details

This 1/8 scale figure is based on the police woman design shown in Digi Police. Much like the cooking apron Yukari, this one is also a resin-cast figure instead of a mass produced one.

Prototype makers: Be-Full

Price: ￥13,800 JPY (before tax), ￥14,904 (after tax)

Pre-order period: July 19th, 2017 12:00 PM (JST) ~ August 7th, 2017 11:59 PM (JST)

Release date: Towards the end of August

Pre-order Links:

Pulchra Online Shop (Please note we’re linking to the Japanese pages for technical reasons. An English version of the website is available but it’s incomplete.)

AHS Online Shop

More to Come!

The pre-order period listed above is actually the first of two pre-order periods opening up. For the first round, there is a limited number of figures available, and they’re only accepting orders on Pulchra’s online shop and the AHS Store

The second round of pre-orders (which they plan to share details for sometime mid-August), however, will not have a limit on the number of available figures, and they plan to utilize a variety of online as well as retail shops.

Ordering on Pulchra

While there is an English version of the Pulchra website and these figures are available on the English version, fans will notice that going to the website will leave you with only an item number to go off of. There’s no name, no picture. Nothing to identify the figure other than the item number. While some fans may be fine with this, we’re providing help ordering in Japanese should you prefer to do it that way.

First, you’ll want to create an account on the English version of Pulchra for easiest use. Otherwise, you’ll have to follow some extra steps in Japanese to order without an account. Follow the steps below to order without an account. To order with an account, simply follow the steps up until you make your account and then you’ll be prompted to login to your Paypal to pay.
