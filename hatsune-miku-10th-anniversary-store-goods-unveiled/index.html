Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Store Goods Unveiled!

marumain April 15, 2017 Merchandise, News

First announced on Miku Day (3/9), the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store in Atre Akihabara is scheduled to open from April 22nd to May 7th, and now, all of the limited-time goods that will be sold at the store have been revealed!

Merch On Sale (April 22nd-May 7th)

Here is the initial round of merchandise that will be available on the store’s opening day, April 22nd:


Acrylic Key Chain / ¥650 JPY each (about $6.50 USD)
Illustrations by 上倉エク

First up, bearing the illustrations of 上倉エク is the special acrylic key chain, priced at ¥650 JPY each. Though it’s Miku’s 10th anniversary that the store is celebrating, all the of the Crypton VOCALOIDs are represented on these goods.

Print Cookies (10 in each box) / ¥1,000 JPY per box (about $10 USD)
Illustrations by 上倉エク

Bearing the same cute designs from 上倉エク are the double-sided print cookies, priced at ¥1,000 JPY per box of 10 cookies! Don’t the Crypton VOCALOIDs look so cute wearing their little aprons that you could just eat them all up? (Their cookies, that is; I was totally talking about their cookies!)


Die-Cut Cushions (about 30 cm long) / ¥2,000 JPY each (about $20 USD)
Illustrations by 上倉エク

We haven’t seen the last of 上倉エク’s adorable designs yet! This time, we get to see them printed (and felt) on die-cut cushions that are not only soft to the touch but also surprisingly sturdy, costing just ¥2,000 JPY a piece.

L-sized Full-Graphic T-shirts / ¥6,500 JPY (about $65 USD)
Illustration by しゅがお

Up next comes the large, full-graphic T-shirt for ¥6,500 JPY showcasing the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store main visual piece by しゅがお! Nothing else tells the world “I love Miku!” quite like having her visage sprawled across your entire being!


75 mm Can Badge / ¥500 JPY each (about $5 USD)
Illustration by しゅがお

If the T-shirt isn’t enough, you can wear even more of しゅがお’s Miku with one (or several) of these special can badges! At just ¥500 JPY a piece, you can easily pick up quite a few for your friends (or all for yourself if you feel so inclined)!


11.5×8 cm Acrylic Stand / ¥1,200 (about $12 USD)
Illustration by しゅがお

Though you can’t wear this one, しゅがお’s Miku makes for a stunning-looking decorative stand that you can take with you and assemble anywhere you like for the perfect showpiece for your Miku fandom!

A4-size Clear File Folders / ¥350 JPY each (about $3.50 USD)
Illustrations by しゅがお, 上倉エク, and なつめえり

All three of the talented artists who are providing the official artwork for the store goods will have their works printed onto clear file folders, priced at ¥350 JPY each! This would be perfect for the school children in Japan who are just starting their new semester!

Merch On Sale (April 29th-May 7th)

Below are the merchandise scheduled to go on sale for the second half of the 10th anniversary store’s opening from April 29th until its closing on May 7th.


75 mm Can Badges / ¥500 JPY (about $5 USD)
Illustrations by なつめえり

なつめえり’s works are getting turned into can badges as well! They are priced at ¥500 JPY, so you can easily collect all six kinds to give to friends (or, again, to keep for yourself)!


15×12 cm Giant Acrylic Key Chain / ¥1,200 JPY (about $12 USD)
Illustration by なつめえり

Yes, you’re reading that correctly. This is a 15×12 cm (about 6×4¾ in.) key chain bearing なつめえり’s Miku design! Priced at ¥1,200 JPY, it won’t be the cheapest or smallest key chain that you’ll own, but it’s certainly one of a kind!


12.1×13.6 cm Mini Colored Paper Collection, 9 sheets per box / ¥450 JYP (about $4.50 USD)
Illustrations by なつめえり and 上倉エク

These colored paper sets contain one of all nine designs, and even come with a free stand that you can decorate, all for just ¥450 JYP each!


51.5×72.8cm Tapestry / ¥3,500 JPY (about $35 USD)
Illustration by なつめえり

And last but not least is this very beautiful piece of tapestry with all six of なつめえり’s Crypton VOCALOIDs! Make sure to think of a big enough spot in your home to hang this beforehand!

Free Stickers

If that wasn’t enough, the Atre Akihabara store is even offering this special deal: For every ¥1,000 JPY (about $10 USD) that you spend on goods at the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store, you will receive a free sticker! There are seven different stickers in all that you can collect, but which ones you will get are determined at random, so you’ll need deep pockets if you aim to collect all seven!

Official Website

Finally, it has also been announced that the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store’s official site is now up! On the site, you can get up-to-date prices and availability of each store item, the latest announcements, and more! Those who are planning on visiting the store come this April 22nd would do well to bookmark this site and visit it frequently!

Of course, don’t forget that you can also stay tuned to VNN for all of your Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary updates!
