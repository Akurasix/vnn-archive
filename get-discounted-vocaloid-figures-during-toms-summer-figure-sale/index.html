Get Discounted VOCALOID Figures During TOM’s Summer Figure Sale!

Hoshi July 13, 2017 Merchandise

Tokyo Otaku Mode is having a huge summer figure sale, with over 30 pages worth of figures and figure accessories! While fans can grab tons of different figures, we all know VNN’s readers want to know about the VOCALOID figures. So, here’s a list of all the VOCALOID goods on sale.

Hatsune Miku

Of course, Miku goods are abundant. You can chill out with the 1/4 Scale Snow Miku figure or buy some Nendoroid Co-de figures if your budget is a bit smaller. And, of course, it wouldn’t be summer without a figure of Miku in a swimsuit as well!

Nendoroid More: Face Swap 02

While you’re out buying Nendoroids, or, if you already have more than enough Nendos, why not get some special face plates for them? The Nendoroid More: Face Swap 02 set comes with 9 different expressions that give your Nendoroids a lot more display options! The set is on sale for $49.67 USD. Please note that these faces may not work for Rin, Len, or KAITO. The three were made before the change in how face plates are made, which will likely make them incompatible.
