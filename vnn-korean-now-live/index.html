VNN Korean is Now Live!

Monds April 7, 2017 Community

For those of you who have been following VNN for some time, you will probably remember that Korean was one of the first languages announced and we had announced it as “coming soon” on multiple occasions. However, due to a few reasons, we have been delaying it for some time. But now, the wait is finally over! A huge thank you to Dr. Yun for helping us make this happen. Keep an eye out on our Twitter for Korean articles to be posted as well.

You can head on over to the Korean version of VNN here.

This will make VNN currently available in 12 languages. Stay tuned for more as we continue to grow!
