VOCALOID Themed Jewelry Now at Tokyo Otaku Mode!

Hoshi September 2, 2017 Merchandise, News

Fans of the Artemis Kings and Miku collaboration rejoice! VOCALOID jewelry pieces based on Miku, Rin, Len, Luka, KAITO, and MEIKO are now available! In addition to the rings and pendants from the initial collab, there are newer pieces as well! While the pieces are expensive, remember that they’re actually fine jewelry. These stylish pieces are the classiest way to show your love of VOCALOID.

In addition to these jewelry pieces, there are also special leather bracelets released for the Summer Festival collaboration. For more information on all those goods, click here.

Please note these items are special order. It’ll take about 8 weeks after you order for them to be ready.
