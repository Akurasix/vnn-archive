New Items Available in the Otomachi Una Online Shop!

R3van December 22, 2017 Merchandise, News 1 Comment 4,814 Views

Back in October, Internet Co. revealed the official online goods shop for their eel-themed VOCALOID, Otomachi Una! There was a very limited selection when it first opened – only keychains and fluffy hats – but now the shop offers more goodies based on Una’s three voicebanks: Sugar, Spicy, and Talk Ex!

Original Goods

Note that all prices include tax. 

Plush Otaman Hat (Sugar): ¥3,980 ($35 USD)

Plush Otaman Hat (Spicy): ¥3,980 ($35 USD)

Plush Otaman Hat (Talk Ex): ¥3,980 ($35 USD)

Acrylic Keychain (Sugar, Spicy, Talk Ex): ¥700 ($6 USD)

Acrylic Keychain (Sugar): ¥700 ($6 USD)

Acrylic Keychain (Spicy): ¥700 ($6 USD)

Acrylic Keychain (Talk Ex): ¥700 ($6 USD)

New Goods

Note that all prices include tax.

Plush Doll (Sugar): ¥2,400 ($21 USD)

Plush Doll (Spicy): ¥2,400 ($21 USD)
Plush Doll (Talk Ex): ¥2,400 ($21 USD)

Phone Case (Talk Ex): ¥4,500 ($40 USD)

Able to fit: iPhone 6, iPhone 7 and Multi-form 

Sticker Set (Sugar): ¥300 ($3 USD)

Sticker Set (Spicy): ¥300 ($3 USD)

Sticker Set (Talk Ex): ¥300 ($3 USD)
Sticker Set (Variety): ¥300 ($3 USD)

Where to Buy

All these items are available for immediate purchase on Otomachi Una’s online shop here. Shipping costs ¥700 ($6 USD) per order. However, those living outside of Japan will have to use a forward / proxy service.

Fans can also take advantage of a coupon for orders over ¥1,000 ($9 USD) by friending Una’s LINE account and sending them the message “グッズクーポン” . You can find and scan their QR code here!

Some of the goodies are going fast, so be sure to order them before they’re sold out!

Source: Otomachi Una Official Twitter
