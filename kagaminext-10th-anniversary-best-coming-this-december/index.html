Pair of Kagamine 10th Anniversary Albums Coming this December!

marumain November 23, 2017 Merchandise, News

As 2017 winds down and the thrill from Hatsune Miku‘s 10th anniversary begins to wane (or not), VOCALOID fans can start drumming up excitement for another pair of 10th anniversaries coming next month: those of Kagamine Len and Kagamine Rin! This VOCALOID pair was first released on December 27th, 2007, and both EXIT TUNES and KarenT are putting together their own albums and accompanying goods to celebrate the big day!

Special Merchandise

-LODESTAR- will also come with its own SONOCA card that will enable you to download a non-stop medley of Rin and Len songs entitled “RINLENMANIA of KARENT LINEUP,” created by Ann-MeltsP. You can learn more about the medley on its official site here, and start listening to it on Niconico here!

Also included on the SONOCA card is the download for a track entitled “第二幕　祝☆10周年誕生祭、開催！〜前夜祭〜 (Act 2: Celebrate☆10th Anniversary Birthday Fest! ~Last Night’s Fest~),” also created by HitoshizukuP as a follow-up to the story from Kagaminext’s SONOCA card.

Finally, ordering at each of the following online stores will net you an exclusive can badge, which differ depending on the store! All illustrations used for the badges were provided by 森 (Mori).

“Kagamine Rin and Len 10th Anniversary -LODESTAR-” costs ￥2,800 JPY (about $25 USD).
