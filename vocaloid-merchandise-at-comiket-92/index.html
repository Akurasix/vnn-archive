VOCALOID Merchandise at ComiKet 92

Jade Kaiba August 4, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News

Comic Market (or ComiKet) is taking place at the Tokyo Big Sight starting today! The semi-annual doujin fair has always been a great place to find VOCALOID merchandise, and ComiKet 92 will be no exception, with various VOCALOID companies and producers offering exclusive goods!

We have provided a rundown of as many of the products and promotions available as we could, as well as details of the event and where to find 1st Place and BIGHEAD below.

1st Place

1st PLACE announced that they will be offering merchandise featuring VOCALOID IA and the characters from Kagerou Project!

Besides selling various goods at the event, 1st PLACE will also be running a special promotion, where a free bonus item will be given to customers who spend over 3,000 JPY on their products.

For more details on the products and promotional items offered, check out the summaries below.

IA Merchandise

1st PLACE have announced that the following IA products will be on sale at this event.

IA Pins

Price: 1,200 JPY

There are 3 types of pins available: IA super-deformed, IA logo and ARIA mark.

IA Necklace

Price: 800 JPY

This dog tag necklace features the IA logo on the front and a silhouette of IA on the back.

IA Sketchbook

Price: 500 JPY

This IA sketchbook was made in collaboration with the stationary company マルマン (Maruman), who have a reputation for excellence in design and quality according to 1st PLACE!

IA Ema

Price: 1,200 JPY

For those who don’t know, an ema is a wooden plaque which Shinto worshipers write their prayers or wishes on. This one features IA in a cute shrine maiden outfit!

IA Coin Case

Price: 800 JPY

This coin case features a cute chibi IA.

Kagerou Project Merchandise

Kagerou Project Rubber Phone Straps

Price: 500 JPY each

These cute rubber phone straps feature the various characters from Kagerou Project in animal themed kigurumi.

Kagerou Project Keychains

Price: 500 JPY each

This assortment of keychains also features the Kagerou Project characters in their animal themed kigurumi. The keychains have a unique star-shaped ring and can also be used as coasters!

Kagerou Project Leather Can Badges

Price: 500 JPY each

These can badges also feature the Kagerou Project characters in their animal themed kigurumi.

Kagerou Project Clear File Folders

Price: 300 JPY each

There’s plenty of choice for these Kagerou Project clear file folders, of which there are 12 different designs to choose from!

Kagerou Project T-Shirts

Price: 2,500 JPY

There are 5 different designs of Kagerou Project T-shirts on offer, which are redesigns of the shirts that proved to be so popular at Niconico Cho Kaigi 2017 that they sold out!

Kagerou Project A2 Posters

Price: 500 JPY each

These Kagerou Project A2 posters come in 2 different styles.

 

Kagerou Project Coin Cases

Price: 800 JPY each

These coin cases feature the Kagerou Project characters in cute animal outfits.

Kagerou Project Acrylic Stands

Price: 1,000 JPY each

This type of acrylic stand actually moves when shaken and comes in 3 different styles!

Kagerou Project Acrylic Badges

Price: 500 JPY each

These acrylic badges come in 9 different styles, featuring the Kagerou Project characters in eared hoodies, designed by the artist しづ  (Shidu).

Shidu ‘Bar Girl’ Sketchbook

Price: 500 JPY

A sketchbook of Shidu’s works, featuring (of all things) a girl with a crowbar!

Ishifuro Illustration Collection, ‘ISHIFURO WORKS’

Price: 1,000 JPY

This art book features a collection of illustrations by producer, Ishifuro.

Kirai-chan Rubber Phone Strap

Price: 500 JPY

This rubber strap features a chibi illustration of Ishifuro’s well-known character, Kirai-chan.

1st Place’s Special Promotion

All customers who purchase over 3,000 JPY worth of products will receive a special bonus in the form of an original memo pad, featuring one of four designs.

Please note that stock is limited so the promotion will end once the memo pads are out of stock.

CielP

CielP‘s Twitter revealed that they will be present on the first day of ComiKet at 11b, East to promote and sell their brand new albums, “Healing” and “Flying,” for 500 JPY each. Illustrations for both album jackets were provided by Natsuki.

Clean Tears

mothy

Finally, mothy, creator of the Evillious Chronicles, can be found at 48b, East on the first day of ComiKet to distribute the long-awaited ‘master of the heavenly yard‘ album, the conclusion the popular series!

‘master of the heavenly yard’ will feature a 15 minutes and 56 second long track that compiles all 12 songs in the long-running series, plus an 8-page short story booklet. The album will be available at ComiKet for 500 JPY, and those who purchase it during the event will also receive a free postcard. See the below tweet for a short teaser!
