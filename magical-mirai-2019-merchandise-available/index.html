Hatsune Miku: Magical Mirai 2019 Merchandise Now Available!

Spookie May 24, 2019 Events, Merchandise, News

It is now time our dear Hatsune Miku goods collectors:Magical Mirai 2019’s official merchandise is now available for purchase via Miku’s official store!

Awesome Goods for Everyone!

This should be no surprise to you, as Mku always offered a huge amount of merchandise for almost every official event, and this year’s Magical Mirai of corse makes no exception to the rule! Starting from now, you will begin to find more items themed after this event: Full Graphic T-shirts, Muffler Towels, Tote-bags and its exclusive penlight are now available! Let’s check them out together!

Full Graphic T-shirts

Let’s start with something everyone will probably love: their Full Graphic T-shirts themed after Magical Mirai!

Each of those T-shirts is priced 5,500 JPY and features an awesome illustration set and design provided by Sogawa! Multiple sizes are available (S/M/L/XL) and will surely bring your support energy out during the event!

You can choose between: Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Kagamine Len, Megurine Luka, KAITO and MEIKO versions of this product!

Click the name of the character you want to purchase and you will be redirected to its products page!

Those product are based off of pre-ordered unit. Once you ordered yours, you cannot cancel the order!

Muffler Towels

Fluffy and adorable, those Muffler Towels make the perfect gift for any fan of Miku and her friends!

Those towels are made in Japan using 100% cotton, their size is 1100x200mm and feature six adorable designs provided by Chisuke! e assure you, their fluffiness matches perfectly with each of their cute illustration! Each of them costs 1,500 JPY.

You can choose between: Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Kagamine Len, Megurine Luka, KAITO and MEIKO versions of this product!

Click the name of the character you want to purchase and you will be redirected to its products page!

Those product are based off of pre-ordered unit. Once you ordered yours, you cannot cancel the order!

Tote-bags

Tote-bags are probably one of the assets that any fan should have and, o satisfy our eager collector desires, this year we got two different variants we can chose between!

Each tote-bag’s size is 480x410x140mm, priced 1,000 JPY and features two different illustrations depending on which variant you chose.

  1. The first one features iXima‘s marvelous Megurine Luka illustration in her Future Circus outfit, alongside ni02‘s Magical Mirai 2019 main visual with Miku.
    You can pre-order it here.
  2. Te second one also features a ni02 illustration, the chibi group one to be precise, but is also accompanied by Zain‘s stylish artwork on the other side.
    You can pre-order it here.

Will you grab one and show it off? They are quite stylish after all!

Penlight

Last but not least, we have Magical Mirai 2019’s official penlight. No concert can be considered a real one without some sparks from the crowd, after all!

Made in ABS, PVC and PC, also featuring ni02’s cute chibi artwork and 3 AAA batteries, this penlight only costs 3,000 JPY! We highly suggest you to buy one as soon as possible, since those usually get sold out pretty quickly, and during the concert only official Hatsune Miku penlights (or similar ones) are allowed!

You can pre-order this penlight by clicking here!

New Merchandise and Exclusive Goods Available at the Venue!

They also started introducing more of their official merchandise, alongside some exclusives you will only find at the venue! Let’s check it out!

Let’s check the price list together:

  • Full Graphic LIVE Happi (Miku or Luka ver.) –6,000 JPY
  • Vynil Pouch (Includes 6 flake seals!) –2,000 JPY
  • Electric Fan –3,800 JPY
  • Sacosh – 4,000 JPY

But there seem to be some exclusive items that you will find only at the venue! Here is the list:

  • Hatsune Miku Magical Mirai 2019 Plush – 4,000 JPY
  • OUTDOOR PRODUCTS Rucksack – 10,000 JPY
  • Glass cloth (set of 3) – 1,000 JPY

I personally fell in love with the plush, I truly wish I could get one!

Capsule toy

Last but not least we are going to see two different capsule toys offers, one by Bandai Inc.and the other byHobby Stock Inc., both featuring some Magical Mirai 2019 prizes!

Each of those capsule toy charge 300 JPY per draw, and there are many prizes (especially from the Bandai one which features mini figures, rubber keychains and can badges!).

Check out the goods’ category on Magical Mirai 2019‘s website for more details!

Sources

All of those items are available via Miku’s Official Goods Store will be shipped in late July, just in time for Miku’s most important event of the year! Let us know if you plan to attend the event and, of course, if you liked those items and bought some!
