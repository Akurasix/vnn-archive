“MIKU With YOU” Concert Updates: Trailer, Tickets, and Merchandise Info!

Jade Kaiba November 16, 2017 Events, News Leave a comment 2,809 Views

The HATSUNE MIKU With YOU 2017 CHINA FESTIVAL concert will be held on November 25th at the Shanghai New International Expo Center! This will be the first live performance using the new Hatsune Miku V4 Chinese voicebank since it was released earlier this year.

See below for a trailer with fans proclaiming “我们爱初音未来！ (We love Hatsune Miku!)” Then read on to find out more details on the concert, tickets, and merchandise!

Concert Details

There will be two concert performances held on the day of MIKU With YOU 2017, one in the afternoon and one in the evening. There will also be an exhibit open at the venue throughout the day from 9:00 am to 7:00 pm.

Venue Location:
E5 Exhibition Space of the Shanghai New International Expo Center

Schedule:
9:00 am: Doors and exhibit open
12:00 pm: Admission for the afternoon concert starts
1:00 ~ 3:00 pm: Afternoon concert
5:30 pm: Admission for the evening concert starts
7:00 pm: Exhibit closes
6:30 ~ 8:30 pm: Evening concert
8:30 ~ 9:30: POPPRO Award Ceremony
10:00 pm: Event concludes

From 12:00 pm to 5:00 pm at the exhibit, they will be taking votes for the best entries in the Hatsune Miku 10th anniversary illustration and photography contest on POPPRO. The theme of this contest is “My 10 Years With Miku,” and attendees can vote for their favorites by scanning their POPPRO QR code. Voting will also be taking place online during the same time frame, and an award ceremony for the winners will be held after the evening concert performance.

Click here to see entries in the illustration contest, and here for entries in the photography contest.

Ticket Details

You can purchase both domestic and overseas tickets to MIKU WITH YOU 2017.

All fans who purchase a concert ticket will also be able to receive an entrance ticket to the POPPRO Award Ceremony. This offer used to be exclusive to fans who purchased a ticket to the evening performance but this is now open to those attending the afternoon concert. However, please note that the Award Ceremony tickets are limited in number so they are offered on a first come first served basis to attendees of the afternoon performance.

Fans who purchase tickets to the evening concert will have the Award Ceremony entrance tickets included in their purchase. Fans who purchase tickets to the afternoon concert will be able to get their entrance ticket at the Shanghai New International Expo Center No.2 entrance service counter.

Check out our guide below on the types of ticket available and how to purchase them.

Types of tickets:

You can purchase SVIP, S1 or S2 tickets.

The SVIP tickets get you a place closest to the stage, S1 tickets get you a place in the center, and S2 tickets get you a place in the section furthest from the stage. Due to the proximity to the stage, SVIP tickets are the most expensive at ￥1480 CNY (about $223 USD), S1 tickets are priced in the mid-range at ￥980 CNY (about $148 USD) and the S2 tickets are the cheapest option at ￥680 CNY (about $103 USD).

How to purchase domestic tickets

To purchase tickets to the concert from within China, simply visit Damai or Taobao‘s Chinese pages.

How to purchase overseas tickets

For those who want to cheer Miku on from outside of China, overseas tickets are also available. Please follow the guidelines below in order to purchase your overseas tickets.

Rules for overseas tickets:

  • Limited to 4 tickets per account.
  • If you do not pay within 15 minutes of purchase, the purchase will be cancelled, so please pay within the alloted time.
  • Payment methods accepted: VISA / MASTERCARD / JCB / American Express etc.

Where to purchase overseas tickets from:

You can purchase tickets from either of the following ticket sales pages.

Damai (English page)
Taobao (English page)

Please note that TAOBAO will only be selling SVIP tickets.

How to obtain overseas tickets:

Customers who purchased tickets through one of the above sites will be required to exchange tickets on the day of the concert, as international tickets can not be processed via international mail.

Exchange location:

Shanghai New International Expo Center No.2 entrance service counter

Exchange date and time:

November 25th from 8:30 am to 7:00 pm

How to exchange:

You will need to bring your passport as proof of identity and present the cell phone number you registered with on the website you purchased from.
