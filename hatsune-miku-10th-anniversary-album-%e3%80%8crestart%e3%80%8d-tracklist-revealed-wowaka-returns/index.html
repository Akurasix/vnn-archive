HATSUNE MIKU 10th Anniversary Album 「Re:Start」 Tracklist Revealed + Wowaka Returns!

Hoshi August 9, 2017 Merchandise, News

The HATSUNE MIKU 10th Anniversary Album 「Re:Start」 has finally had it’s tracklist revealed! In addition to old favorites, there are new songs coming as well. In total, this 2-Disc album features 24 fan-favorite producers! In addition, the album features Wowaka in a way many fans wouldn’t expect. After a long hiatus, Wowaka has returned to VOCALOID music! Track 1 of CD 1 is a brand new song by the famous VOCALOID producer!

Tracklist

As mentioned earlier, this album comes with two discs. Each disc contains 12 songs. While Disc 1 consists of brand new songs, Disc 2 is all about the old favorites.

Wowaka Returns!

As mentioned before, Wowaka is making a brand new song for this special 10th Anniversary album. The last VOCALOID song Wowaka posted was “Unhappy Refrain” in May 2011. However, after 6 long years, he’s returning to VOCALOID music! Track 1 on Disc 1 is a brand new composition called “Unknown · Mother Goose” made especially for this album. There aren’t any other details available on this song yet, but stay tuned!

Other New Songs

While most of the new songs haven’t been revealed yet, two of them are available from the CD 1 tracklist. DECO*27’s “Hibana” and 40mp’s “Initial Song” are both available on Youtube and NND!
