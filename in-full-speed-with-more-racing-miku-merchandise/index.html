In Full Speed With More Racing Miku Merchandise!

Spookie June 13, 2019 Merchandise, News Leave a comment 1,754 Views

No matter in what period of the year we are in, it’s always time for more Racing Miku merchandise! Isn’t it?

COCOLLABO Brings us an Acrylic Stand and a Parka!

Let’s begin with COCOLLABO‘s new items themed after our current Hatsune Miku GT Project mascot from Goodsmile Racing: Racing Miku 2019! Today, they have added two new different items to their online shop, a big acrylic stand and a comfy parka! Check them out below!

Racing Miku 2019 Thailand Ver. Big Acrylic Stand

This marvelous acrylic stands is bigger than average. It is about 200mm of height and features our beloved Racing Miku 2019 in her Thailand support attire!

Size is W230xH200xD5mm, with the base having a diameter of 130mm! Its price is 3,240 JPY and will be released on early September!

Want to pre-order this item? Then click here to reach its product page!

Racing Miku 209 Full Graphic Parka

Nothing shows your support for a team more than wearing their mascot everyday, isn’t it? I bet this Racing Miku 2019 Full Graphic Parka will look great in your wardrobe then!

Available in three different sizes (M/L/XL) and being made in polyester, this Parka sure doesn’t fail in showing Racing Miku 2019’s charm and enthusiasm!

It is priced 9,720 JPY and will be delivered on early September, just like the previous item!

You can pre-order it by clicking here!

POP UP PARADE Racing Miku 2010 Now up for Pre-order!

Next we have the much awaited POP UP PARADE figure of Racing Miku 2010 from Good Smile Company! It will finally be available for pre-order through their online store!

Miku from the 2010 Hatsune Miku GT Project!

“The fifth figure in the POP UP PARADE series is Racing Miku 2010 Ver. from the 2010 Racing Miku GT Project! Illustrator redjuice‘s mechanic style design has been faithfully recreated, including Miku’s unique heels, her tools and her adorable smile. The figure has been painted to accurately reflect the different textures each material, making for a truly stunning appearance.”

She’s approximately 170mm in height and is priced 3,900 JPY, a real deal for fans in search for high quality figures at an affordable price!

You can find and pre-order her here until July 11th at 12PM (JST)! She’s scheduled for release on October 2019!

Hatsune Miku GT Project Exhibition Period Extended!

Last but not least, we have some great news for fans that are currently in Japan: Hatsune Miku GT Project’s exhibition period got extended to June 22nd!
