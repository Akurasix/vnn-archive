Winter Wonder Festival 2017 Figure Roundup!

Hoshi February 19, 2017 Events, Merchandise Leave a comment 4,019 Views

February 19th in Japan marked Winter Wonder Festival 2017, the winter version of the biannual figure convention. Everyone from Good Smile Company to garage kit makers were there to show off figures, and man, did the figure companies have a ton to offer! While there was quite a bit of Miku, we also saw almost all of the Crypton VOCALOIDs and even Yukari represented!

Of course, Miku was the star of the show. Thanks to Mikufan, we have all the announced Miku figures from WonFes! Picture sources are linked via figure name.

In addition to the Crypton VOCALOIDs, Yukari made an appearance, too! Pulchra revealed the prototype for her Police Woman figure!

Racing Yukari!

However, that wasn’t all from Yukari. While her figure presence was a bit lacking this WonFes, she was far from underrepresented. Most noteworthy of Yukari’s appearances was the Racing Yukari booth.

The Yuzuki Yukari Racing team had their booth set up and ready to go at precisely 8:42 AM (JST) on February 19th, 2017.

Of course, the team was hard at work preparing for the event well before that! The day before, they revealed the pink and purple Racing Yukari suit that a staff member would wear.
