marumain December 15, 2016 Merchandise, News

Macne fans, rejoice! As of today, all three of Macne Nana’s V4 voice banks are now on sale in both physical boxed form and digital download!

The boxed versions can be purchased separately for ￥10,800 JPY (about $108 USD) each. They can also be purchased together in the “VOCALOID 4 Macne Nana Complete Package” for ￥22,800 JPY (about $228 USD), or in the “VOCALOID 4 Macne Nana Starter Pack” for ￥28,800 JPY (about $288 USD)!

Their digital download versions are only available separately for ￥8,000 JPY (about $80 USD) each. The “VOCALOID 4 Macne Nana Complete Package” and “VOCALOID 4 Macne Nana Starter Package” are not available for digital download.

Finally, each purchase comes equipped with the following AH-Software products:
