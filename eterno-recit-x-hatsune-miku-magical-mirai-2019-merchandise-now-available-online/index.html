ETERNO RÉCIT x Hatsune Miku: Magical Mirai 2019 Merchandise Now Available Online!

Spookie September 3, 2019 Merchandise, News

Hatsune Miku‘s Magical Mirai 2019 might have met its end, but for the fans that could not attend the event, it is now time to rejoice! ETERNO RÉCIT‘s special merchandise of our beloved diva and her friends is, in fact, now available online through their store!

What Can You Buy?

All of the goods are illustrated by Youno and were previously only available at the event’s venue, however, now you can also order them online!

商品の詳細につきましては各ページをご確認ください。

If you were looking for a wallet, fancy rectangular can badges, stands and folding fans portraying your favorite virtual singers from Crypton Future Media, this is your time to shine!

Wallets

Let’s begin with their fancy wallets. They’re probably one of the items that got us interested almost immediately through their tweet!

Made with PU leather, this fancy wallet will allow you to store cards and money at ease! It is only priced at 9,000 JPY and available in six different designs, one for each character!

Pick your favorite character by clicking on their name from the list below!

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Kagamine Rin
  • Kagamine Len
  • Megurine Luka
  • MEIKO
  • KAITO

Those wallets are a limited edition product and will be available until October 1st, with a shipping date planned depending on when you manage to order them:

  • Ordered in between of September 2nd – 9th: Shipping in late September.
  • Ordered in between of September 10th – 30th: Shipping in late October.

All of your friends will absolutely envy your new wallet!

Rectangular Can Badges

Next we have six amazing rectangular can badges, each portraying one of the Piapro characters holding a folding fan with grace!

Those badges have a size of 8 x 5.5cm each and will be yours for only 700 JPY per badge!

All of the above designs are available, all you have to do is pick your favorite character from the list below!

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Kagamine Rin
  • Kagamine Len
  • Megurine Luka
  • MEIKO
  • KAITO

Which one will you choose? Or are you maybe aiming to get them all? Tell us in the comment section below!

“Folding Fan” Acrylic Stands

Right after those can badges, we find some amazing character stands as well!

The stand’s height varies depending on the character and the price is 1,900 JPY each. Click on the character’s name from the above list to get yours!

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Kagamine Rin
  • Kagamine Len
  • Megurine Luka
  • MEIKO
  • KAITO

Just like the wallets, these stands will be available until October 1st, with a shipping date planned depending on when you order them:

  • Ordered in between of September 2nd – 9th: Shipping in late September.
  • Ordered in between of September 10th – 30th: Shipping in late October.

We are sure you will love to exhibit them all together! Or even just your favorite one, of course!

Folding Fans

Last but not least, we have those amazingly designed folding fans, each based off of either Miku and her friends, bringing an elegant vibe to you!

Each of those designs is made in silk and bamboo and shows off a different flower motif based off of the character you chose:

  • Hatsune Miku: Peonia Suffruticosa
  • Kagamine Rin: Japanese Morning Glory
  • Kagamine Len: Sunflower
  • Megurine Luka: Japanese Wisteria
  • MEIKO: Chinese Mahogany
  • KAITO: Hydrangea Macrophylla

There are priced 2,700 JPY each! You can find them by clicking here, and remember to mark which character you want!

These fans are a limited edition product, so you may want to grab yours fast before they stop manufacturing them!
