VOCALOID 4 Library Xin Hua Chinese and Japanese Versions Now on Sale!

marumain September 27, 2017 News Leave a comment 3,557 Views

Xin Hua‘s long-awaited V4 update and brand new Japanese voice bank are now available for purchase on Shanghai Wangcheng’s Taobao and Gynoid’s official online store!

Available Versions

Chinese Digital Download Edition

VOCALOID 4 Library Xin Hua Chinese Version is the only version that is currently available for digital download.

Purchase the Digital Download Edition on Shanghai Wangcheng’s Taobao for ¥298.00 RMB (about $45.00 USD)

Chinese Physical Edition

The Physical Edition of VOCALOID 4 Library Xin Hua Chinese Version contains her voice bank on a DVD-ROM, and the initial batch will come bundled with the album 華彩 (Hua Cai).

Purchase the Physical Edition on Shanghai Wangcheng’s Taobao for ¥598.00 RMB (about $90.00 USD)

Japanese Regular Edition

The Regular Edition of VOCALOID 4 Library Xin Hua Japanese Version contains her voice bank, plus a special sticker set.

Purchase the Regular Edition on the Gynoid Official Store for ￥10,800 JPY (about  $97.00 USD).
Purchase the Regular Edition on Amazon

Japanese Cubase AI Bundle Edition

The Cubase AI Bundle Edition of VOCALOID 4 Library Xin Hua Japanese Version contains her voice bank, VOCALOID 4 Editor for Cubase, and a set of three large-sized stickers.

Purchase the Cubase AI Bundle Edition on the Gynoid Official Store for ￥18,360 (about $164.00 USD)
Purchase the Cubase AI Bundle Edition on Amazon

System Requirements

DVD-ROM drive (for physical purchases)
Internet Connection
Windows: Windows 10 / 8.1 / Windows 8 / Windows 7 (32 / 64 bit)
Mac: OS X 10.12 (Cubase 9 and Cubase 8 only) / OS X 10.11 (Cubase 9 and Cubase 8 only) / OS X 10.10 (incompatible with Cubase 7, Cubase Artist 7) / OS X 10.9 (incompatible with Cubase Pro 8.5, Cubase Artist 8.5) / OS X 10.8 (incompatible with the Cubase 8 series) (32 / 64 bit)

CPU: Intel Dual Core CPU or greater
RAM: 8GB or more recommended (minimum of 4GB required)

Recommended Tempo: 80 ~ 200BPM
Recommended Range: G#2 ~ E4

Compatible DAW Software: Cubase PRO 8, Cubase Artist 8, Cubase Element 8, Cubase AI 8, Cubase LE 8, Cubase 7.5, Cubase Artist 7.5, Cubase 7, Cubase Artist 7, Cubase Element 7, Cubase AI 7, Cubase LE 7

Audio Device (Windows): DirectX or ASIO (Gynoid strongly recommends ASIO)

Official Illustrations and Logo

Gynoid is also distributing Xin Hua’s official VOCALOID 4 logo, concept art, and box art by VOFAN and ZARD for fans to download and use however they like, provided that they are not used for commercial purposes. Fans who wish to use them for commercial purposes will have to get Gynoid’s official permission.
