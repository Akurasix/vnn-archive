Kagamine 10th Anniversary Celebration Updates!

marumain December 20, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News Leave a comment 2,361 Views

In our first article about the 10th anniversary website for Kagamine Len and Kagamine Rin, we mentioned that there would be a lot of updates to report on between the site’s opening and the actual day of their anniversary – December 27th – and we weren’t kidding! Read on for all of the news surrounding the almost one-decade-old VOCALOID pair!

Kagamine 10th Anniversary Shop at Ikebukuro Station!

From December 24th through 27th, a special Kagamine 10th Anniversary Shop will be open for business at the Ikebukuro JR Station in Tokyo! The shop will be selling exclusive merchandise for not just Rin and Len, but also for Hatsune Miku using illustrations that were provided by iXima. Customers who purchase at least ￥3,000 JPY (about $27 USD) (excluding tax) worth of goods will even receive a Kagamine Rin & Len mini handkerchief while supplies last!

Finally, Fujimi Dragonbook has announced a new publication called “Hatsune Miku TRPG: COCORO SESSION feat. Kagamine Rin & Len,” which looks like the follow-up to the original “Hatsune Miku TRPG: COCORO DUNGEON.”

Story from the official site (translation is not official):

“In this world, everyone holds a special song in their hearts. Normal people cannot hear these songs. The only ones who can are music spirits known as “Otodama,” and the “Otodama Wizards” who befriend them… as well as the monsters known as “Otokui” who consume these songs of the heart.”

“Hatsune Miku TRPG is a tabletop role-playing game where you can borrow the powers of an Otodama, and go on adventures in order to defeat the Otokui!”

“In the second edition, ‘COCORO SESSION,’ we’ve added rules that enable you to spend time even outside of the Cocoro Dungeons.”

“So deepen the bonds with your comrades, and go save those who have lost the songs in their hearts!”

This new edition also boasts 80 news songs, such as Karakuri Pierrot, Jabberwocky Jabberwocka, RIP=RELEASE, and many more that lend power to your characters. 5 songs in particular – Kami Hikouki, Servant of Evil, Alien Alien, Senbonzakura, and Last Battle – serve as the motif for 5 new Cocoro Dungeons that await you!

When creating your characters, you have the choice of four “cores” personalities, each of which has its own Otodama to control, and it’s own illustration. From left-to-right, top-to-bottom, these “cores” are Dark, Love, Eccentric, and Melancholic. They were illustrated by NEGI (who also illustrated the new comics), 猫魚 (Nekosakana), ヒライユキオ (Yukio Hirai), and 美和野らぐ (Ragu Miwano).
