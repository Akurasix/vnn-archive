The Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store in LUCUA 1100 Opens Today!

marumain June 28, 2017 Events, Merchandise, News Leave a comment 1,796 Views

With the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store in Atre Akihabara, the HATSUNE MIKU METAL EDITION x Don Quijote Collaboration Shop, the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary PPP-Party in AEON, and most recently, the Hatsune Miku Summer Festival in Shibuya Marui, 2017 is becoming the year for fans of both Miku and shopping!

Today, another new stop on the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary celebratory store chain has officially opened for business: the Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary Collaboration Store in LUCUA 1100!

Event Period: June 28th (Wed.) through July 18th (Tues.)
Event Location: isetan Closet on the 4th floor of LUCUA 1100 in Osaka

The main visual piece for the event was illustrated by the artist ぶーた (Booota), and additional illustrations were provided by にわ子 (Niwako) and たま (Tama). Throughout the event, numerous store-exclusive goods featuring the works of each artist will be on sale!

Special Event Cafe

  • Cafe Business Period: June 28th (Wed.) through July 13th (Thurs.)
  • Cafe Hours: 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM (Last orders made at 10:30 PM) (JST)
  • Location: Bar Del Sole at Osaka Station City

Even after you’re done shopping, you can continue your Hatsune Miku 10th Anniversary store festivities at the newly-opened event cafe at the Bar Del Sole in the plaza connecting the LUCUA 1100 to Osaka Station City!

Miku’s 10th anniversary on August 31st, 2017, edges closer and closer by the day! Stay tuned to VNN as more of the festivities leading up to the big day continue!
