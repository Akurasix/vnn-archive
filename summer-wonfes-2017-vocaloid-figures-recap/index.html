Summer WonFes 2017 VOCALOID Figures Recap!

Hoshi July 30, 2017 Events, Merchandise

It’s that time of year again where figure companies from all over Japan meet in the biggest biannual figure convention in Japan. While fans from all over the world attend this event, even those at home can join in. The #wf2017s hashtag is where all the WonFes action is at. However, please note that scrolling through isn’t safe for work. While WonFes is full of pure and innocent figures, there are also quite a few lewd ones that some fans may not want to see. Of course that’s where VNN comes in! Our WonFes summaries bring you all the VOCALOID goodness without any sifting through Twitter. So let’s get to our Summer WonFes 2017 VOCALOID figures recap!
