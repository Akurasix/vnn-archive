“Mekakucity Actors” Soundtrack Now Available for Download

marumain August 31, 2016 News

As reported on 1st Place’s official Facebook page, the soundtrack for Mekakucity Actors (the anime series for which Jin handled the screenplay), titled MEKAKUCITY M’s, is now available for purchase and download on the iTunes store.

Besides the expected Mekakucity Actors anime series’ opening theme song “Daze” and ending theme “Days”, the soundtrack also includes various songs from the Kagerou Project and BGM tracks from the anime series, 76 tracks in total over two albums. Purchase of one of the full albums (MEKAKUCITY M’s 1 or MEKAKUCITY M’s 2) is $19.99 USD, though each track can also be purchased individually for $0.99 USD each. If you live in a different country, iTunes should automatically redirect you and give you the cost for your own country.
