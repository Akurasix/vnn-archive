IA’s 1st Live Concert in Shanghai “PARTY A GO-GO” Ticket Pre-Orders are Now Open!

Louis July 7, 2016 Events, News Leave a comment 1,484 Views

IA’s 1st Live Concert in Shanghai “PARTY A GO-GO” will be happening in Shanghai, China! The concert is now confirmed to be on the 27th of August at Shanghai’s QSW Convention Center. The setlist has not yet been revealed, but 1st Place has mentioned that she will sing popular songs from Kagerou Project. Preorders for concert tickets are now available at 票虫网. X-Live has also uploaded a teaser for the live concert on Bilibili.

How to Purchase Tickets 
  • VIP: ￥680 RMB ($101.80 USD)
  • Regular: ￥380 RMB ($56.90 USD)

Tickets for VIP are limited to only 100, and VIP ticket holders will get early access to the concert. Fans who purchase VIP tickets will also receive special merchandise for the concert by redeeming it at the “VIP’s redeem station” at the venue. 1st Place has not yet announced details for the concerts merchandise, so stay tuned for our next article about IA’s “PARTY A-GO-GO” concert in Shanghai!

For more information about the concert, you may check out IA’s official website, 1st Place’s official Weibo account, and X-Live’s Weibo, as they will also deliver instant updates about Shanghai’s live concert (Chinese).
