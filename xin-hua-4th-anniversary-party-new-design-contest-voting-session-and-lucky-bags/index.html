Xin Hua 4th Anniversary Party, New Design Contest Voting Session and Lucky Bags!

Spookie January 27, 2019 Community, Events, Merchandise, News

It has been a while since we talked about Xin Hua, the first VOCALOID ever developed in Taiwan. Time surely flies and we are now very close to her 4th anniversary as well, and her developers decided to held a project named “2019 starts with the heart” in her honor . To celebrate her birthday, numerous events have been held, including a contest for a new official design as well as production of some Lucky Bags containing Xin Hua merchandise. Let’s check them out together!

Starting 2019 Anew With a Brand New Design

We know very well that everyone should start the new year with fresh air, and that’s exactly what Xin Hua’s creators wanted to do when announcing a new Design Contest for her back in December 2018. Now they selected several designs as finalists, however the ones that will have the last word on the winner will be the fans.

Posted by 心華Official on Friday, January 25, 2019

With a total of 62 entries, it’s up to fans now to decide which design will win the contest by voting on a Google Forms document. You need to be logged in your Google account to cast your vote and you can only give 5 votes daily (you can vote 5 different designs or even just one 5 times, if you wish so, but only 5 daily votes). Selections will end on February 20th so be sure to support your favorite artists by voting them!

You can vote for your favorite(s) designs daily by clicking here!

Birthday Party

As part of Xin Hua Project 2019 (“2019 starts with the heart”, a word play on her name), this little Virtual Singer will also have a birthday party during Fancy Frontier (FF33), the biggest Doujinshi Conventions in Taiwan, that will be held between February 16th and 17th and there will also be some secret guests! For those who can’t attend the event, however, do not despair! They will also held a Party in Shanghai at the same time as in Taiwan! So you can participate for sure in case you are around here!

Merchandise for everyone!

Let’s no forget every event wouldn’t be a real one without some merchandise for the fans! In fact, on Taobao you can now order a Xin Hua Lucky bag! But it’s not a simple Lucky Bag, of course! There are many different options and all the “rewards” are fixed, so you actually already know what you will get. Here’s some of the merchandise you can get:

Prices of course will change based on the type of bag you choose, but if you are a real Xin Hua fan you will love the offer! Below you can find a list of the various Lucky Bags types available:

  • Type A (Price: RMB 38, about 6 USD): A badge set (3 pins) + a pendant
  • Type B (Price: RMB 128, about 19 USD): a Xin Hua 4th Anniversary T-shirt + + a badge set (3 pins) + a pendant
  • Type C (Price: RMB 268, about 40 USD): Type A Package + a Digital version of one Xin Hua voicebank (Japanese OR Chinese) + a Card Holder
  • Type D (Price: RMB 678, about 100 USD): Type C Package + a Xin Hua 4th Anniversary T-shirt
  • Type E (Price: RMB 1188, about 176 USD): Xin Hua Voicebanks x2 (you get both Japanese and Chinese) + a Xin Hua 4th Anniversary T-shirt + a badge set (3 pins) + an Acrylic Keychain + a Card Holder

Conclusions

And you? Will you celebrate Xin Hua as well? Those merchandises are really good and the party will be as well, but the most important thing is giving her support and love, and we can totally do that together!
